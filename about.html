About Anchor

Impacting lives,
transforming businesses across Africa.

Anchor Resource Management & Trading Ltd is an integrated business development services and trading company based in Ghana, with strategic partnerships across Africa and beyond. We support organisations, entrepreneurs and communities to unlock inclusive, sustainable growth through advisory, finance, trade and project implementation.

Founded in 2019 and incorporated in 2021, Anchor combines deep local knowledge with Pan-African networks to help clients meet global standards while positively impacting lives.

Our motto

“Impacting Lives, Transforming Businesses.”

Excellence and sustainable development through social inclusion define who we are and how we work.

Our story

From advisory partner to Pan-African enabler

Founded in May 2019 as Anchor Resource Management and formalised as Anchor Resource Management & Trading Ltd in 2021, Anchor has grown into a trusted partner for businesses, development partners and communities. We provide integrated business development, advisory and trading solutions that connect clients to finance, markets and capacity-building opportunities.

Vision

Pan-African hub of excellence

To be a Pan-African hub of excellence and one-stop partner, driving inclusive and sustainable transformation in indigenous companies while promoting equitable growth and environmental stewardship.

Mission

Delivering world-class, inclusive solutions

To provide unparalleled consultancy services and innovative products through research, capacity building and innovation—creating sustainable value for all stakeholders and advancing financial and market accessibility across Africa.

Our objective

Scaling impact by 2032

Anchor aims to support thousands of businesses with access to markets, capital and capacity-building, delivering measurable social, environmental and economic impact across the continent.

Industry experience

Multi-sector expertise, Pan-African reach

Anchor’s work spans agribusiness, financial services, manufacturing, tourism and hospitality, energy, education, technology and more. We specialise in locally sourced, non-GMO agro-based value-added products and provide tailored trading, finance and market linkage solutions across West Africa and beyond.

  • Agribusiness & value chains
  • Business & financial advisory
  • Corporate finance & M&A
  • Commodities trading & exports
  • Data protection & privacy
  • Human resource management
  • International trade & market entry
  • Manufacturing & operations
  • Project and programme management
  • Sustainable development & tourism

Anchor has collaborated with institutions such as USAID, GIZ, the National Communications Authority, GIFEC, the Chamber of Agribusiness Ghana and other partners to drive impactful growth and foster sustainable development.
