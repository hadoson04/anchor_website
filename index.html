What we stand for

We work at the intersection of enterprise development, inclusive finance, trade promotion and impact delivery — helping ideas scale into sustainable, measurable outcomes.

The problem

The challenge we address

Across Africa, many small and growing businesses, social enterprises and community initiatives face barriers to growth — limited access to finance, weak market readiness, inadequate capacity and poor linkage to trade and investment opportunities.

At the same time, development partners and impact investors require credible, locally grounded partners who can design, implement and manage programmes that deliver measurable and sustainable impact.

Anchor bridges this gap by facilitating access to finance, strengthening enterprise capacity, enabling trade and supporting project implementation across sectors and communities.
